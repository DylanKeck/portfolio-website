Portfolio Page Goals

Purpose, Audience and Goals

Purpose: This project's purpose is to be a professional portfolio to show potential future employers my skills in fullstack web development.

Audience: This site will be designed for future employers looking for the right fit for their company.

Goals: The primary goal of this site will be to acquire a full time job and attract potential recruiters with my website.

Persona

Name: Jacky Jackson

Age: 48

Role

Jackson is the senior developer at Sandia Labs. He is tasked with hiring new junior developers to join his dev team. He has been working there for the last 10yrs and knows exactly what he wants his devs to be able to accomplish with technical and soft skills.

Technology

  • His work laptop is a Lenovo ThinkPad.
  • He uses a Samsung Galaxy S25 as his daily phone.
  • At home, he uses a Dell XPS 16 laptop.

Attitudes and Behaviors

Jackson has a packed schedule with projects he's working on as well as sorting through all the applications he has received. He will not even look at a website if its structure is a mess. He will look for projects that match the type of projects him and his team work on and if the languages used match up.

Frustrations

Jackson is tired of these potential candidates not having a website that showcases their skills quickly. He is also tired of websites that don't have a set structure making their portfolio easy to read.

Goals

  • Efficiently look at portfolios.
  • Determine at a quick glance the skills a candidate has.
  • Be able to get all info in one place about the candidate.
  • Make a decision quickly if the person gets an interview.

User Story

  • As a user, I want to be able to quickly see what skills and projects the candidate has to offer.
  • As a user, I want to know if they have knowledge of the languages and libraries we use at our company.
  • As a user, I want a quick way to contact with the candidate and proceed further.
